Common Questions

How soon can I start operating?

After your application is approved and payment is processed, you will receive your digital assets and online training modules within 48 hours. Physical stock delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.

Do I need a medical license?

While a medical background is beneficial, it is not required for Starter or Pro levels. We provide comprehensive clinical protocol training. However, for specialized medical services, we recommend partnering with a certified practitioner.

Can I run this as a side-business?

The Starter Associate level is designed for entrepreneurs looking for a flexible side-income. Pro and Enterprise levels typically require full-time commitment or dedicated management staff.
